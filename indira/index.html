MOST RECENT PROJECT
Indira Foundation had a three-day project dated on the 5th, 6th and 7th of January 2020 tagged "Fight Back Malaria", 2020. This project was carried out in two local gorvernment in imo state; Orsu LGA and Mbano LGA.
The target of the "Fight Back Malaria" project was to distribute over 500 treated mosquito nets to children below the age of 5, pregnant women and nursing mothers and this was achieved.
